About Me

Having spent my childhood in Santa Barbara, I’ve always had a connection to surf, sand and let’s be real…small town living. At a young age, I just couldn’t quit my fascination with sports but it was my high school + college years as a Volleyball player that solidified both my passions and overall drive in life. My eclectic tastes have led me through many industries from operations to hospitality, and film to present day, recruiting. Feast your eyes on my new endeavor and the long (but awesome) road ahead.
